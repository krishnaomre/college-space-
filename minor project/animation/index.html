UDAYA (Summer-Internship-Competition) 2024 The School of Business proudly hosted "Udaya - The Summer Internship Competition" on January 20, 2024, bringing together a diverse group of students from 9 universities. Participants passionately shared their experiences, offering valuable insights into their roles within organizations dedicated to societal advancement. The competition culminated with the announcement of winners, recognizing Priyankar Rudra Paul (K.R. Mangalam) in the 1st position with a cash prize of 10,000 and Sabhya Sharma (Sushant University) securing the 2nd position with a cash prize of 5,000. Heartfelt congratulations to these exceptional students! .
